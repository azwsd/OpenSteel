About me

Hey there! I'm an open-source project, built with love by Ahmed Mohamed Ragab, and my mission is simple, to help you view and explore DSTV (.nc) files with ease.

If you work in steel detailing, CNC machining, or steel manufacturing, I'm here to make your life easier. Just load up a .nc file, and I'll display its contents.
